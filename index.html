How do you determine music artist popularity?

The measure of an artist's popularity is often subjective, with different individuals assessing it based on
various factors such as streaming numbers, collaborations with other artists, or international rankings.
By visually analyzing these statistics, we can aim to introduce an element of objectivity into the evaluation of an artist's popularity.


By Total Stream Count

For instance, a bar graph comparing the top 50 artists by their lead streaming
numbers can provide a clear indication of their relative "popularity."
Lead streams is defined by the total number of streams an artist gets from
tracks available inside their personal page’s sections Albums, Singles and Compilations.
By examining this graph, we can observe that Drake has the highest total number
of streams on Spotify, leading some to consider him as the most popular artist.


By Total Number of Collaborations

Through the exploration of this network graph, we can explore the connections between artists
through their collaborations on popular songs. Each line represents collaborations that happened
in the music industry. These features and connections can significantly impact an artist's overall
demand and influence. The artists in this graph are part of the top 50 artists who have the most collaborations.
The reasoning behind this is to show who has connections with people who have a lot of connections.
As you can see in this graph, Nicki Minaj has the most links with other collaborating artists.
According to this definition of popularity, she would be deemed the most popular artist.

By International Rankings

Furthermore, this choropleth displaying the distribution of Spotify streams worldwide can be used as a metric.
The density of colors on the map corresponds to the concentration of streams in different regions. 
This raises questions about the relationship between international fame and popularity.
This graph shows the top 5 popular songs' artist(s) per region. Arianna Grande’s songs
ranks highly in numerous countries and would be considered “popular” in an international viewpoint.
Therefore, in an international setting, Arianna Grande would be considered the most popular artist.

While acknowledging that popularity remains subjective, visual indicators can assist in determining who you personally perceive as
the most popular artist. Your assessment may be influenced by a combination of factors or a personal weighting of specific criteria.
Ultimately, what matters most is the artist you love and consider popular according to your own judgment.
